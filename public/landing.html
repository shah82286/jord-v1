02 — The Player Moment

Hit your shot. Scan your ball. Watch it land.

Each ball has a 6-character code on a QR sticker. Players scan, GPS locks the position, the system auto-detects fairway, rough, or out of bounds, and a satellite flyover replay plays full-screen.

No app to download. The whole flow takes about twelve seconds.
