A JORD-aware help agent built into every page.

Tap the 🆘 Help button anywhere in the admin console to chat with an AI agent that knows JORD's products and your event's current state — "How do I refund a player?", "Where do I upload my sponsor logos?", "Why is my pairings poster blank?".
