How to do a full backyard test

1
Scan the Admin QR at the top of this page with your phone. Log in with your password.
2
In admin → Course map, search your home address. Place a Tee box marker at one end of your yard. Place the Pin at the other end. Draw a fairway polygon around the space. Save the map.
3
In admin → Ball pool, add some codes (e.g. TEST01, TEST02). Share the Registration QR with yourself and register a player on your phone.
4
Option A — Real GPS: Go to /scan/CODE on your phone and physically stand somewhere in your yard. GPS will calculate actual distance from the tee.
4
Option B — Simulate: Use the map above to click a spot, enter your code, generate the test link. Open it on your phone — GPS is pre-loaded from your click.
5
Watch the Leaderboard update live. Open it in another browser tab on your computer.
