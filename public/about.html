The Live Map

Every shot lands on a satellite view of the hole.

Tap "Map" on the leaderboard and it expands full-screen with every ball drop visible. Painted fairway, rough, and OOB zones underneath. Tap any team in the strip below to filter to just their dots. The whole event watches drives stack up on the same hole, in real time.

  • Color-coded zones — Fairway, Rough, OOB, Green
  • One ball drop per drive — tap to see player + yards

  • Sharp ESRI imagery — every blade of grass visible

The Player Experience

Hit your shot. Scan the ball. Watch it land.

Each ball has a unique 6-character code on a tiny QR sticker. Players scan, GPS locks (3-meter accuracy), the system auto-detects fairway/rough/OOB, and a flyover replay plays full-screen. The whole thing is faster than walking off your shot.

  • Pure web — no app downloads, works on iPhone & Android
  • OTP-style 6-digit ball code, paste-friendly, auto-advance
  • Auto zone detection from the course you mapped
  • Optional rangefinder override for Closest to Pin
The Operator's View

The Monitor dashboard shows every shot as it lands. Filter to a single team. Spot-correct a bad GPS lock with two clicks. See every team's status at a glance — who's submitted, who hasn't, who's leading. No spreadsheets, no walkie-talkies.

Setup in Five Minutes
